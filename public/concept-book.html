03 · The meme

School of the Future, made coherent

Tonight resolved the aesthetic tension Shaw named — high-tech temperature vibes versus class vibes — into one world: the School of the Future. Not Harry Potter, because it's AI, not magic. The Last Human Demo Day is the event held there; the temperature meme is the overarching feel (the creativity direction of intelligence, the exponential answer to HF0's subtraction) and no longer needs literal instantiation in every room. Three layers hold every act.

The frame: one last day of school

Every mechanic already speaks school: enrollment (the link), first bell (the doors), sorting (houses), class periods (the rooms), extra credit (the quests), report cards (the sorting output and the guest state), detention slips (the jar roasts), finals (the stage), the class photo (the broadcast), commencement (graduation). Nothing needs a new metaphor — the building supplies all of it, and the wayfinding kit was already printed in this idiom. Acts III–VI are periods, not a sequence: any order, attendance stamped, all open all night, so late arrivals and stage-lull loops both work.

The spine: five relations to the machine

The reason the rooms cohere instead of reading as disconnected gags: each one stages a different relation between a human and an AI, in escalating order — sorted (it classifies you), jarred (it replaces you), amplified (it multiplies you), treated (it consoles you), judged (it scores you). Walk the periods and you've walked the argument of the event's own name. The stage is the fifth rung — and tonight it got its true school-theme name: the talent show. Pitches as acts, variety-show register, the anti-Hunger-Games staged as the most cooperative format a school owns.

The economy: temperature as currency

The temperature meme stops being decoration and becomes the game loop: every completed period raises your temperature (creativity accrues; the report card shows it climbing), the auditorium is the hottest room in the building by design and by July, and the plunge is the only way down — which is exactly why it's the overtime penalty for founders. Guests spend the night heating up; discipline is the scarce resource. One sentence for the poster: they do subtraction, we do exponential — and the thermostat is a bathtub.

04 · The journey, filmed

Eight acts, one night

The Friday skeleton first — tonight's schedule decision, built around New York arrival physics and a 90-minute show — then the acts as a storyboard. Each frame is a scene a guest will remember and a shot the documentation crew should plan for.

ACT II · THE SCHOOLFirst bell. Three doors carved in stone have sorted people here since 1930; your phone credential opens them tonight.
ACT III · PERIOD 1 — SORTEDThe AI classifies you. Short quiz, a house, one screenshotable card. Extra credit: stamped. Temperature: +0.2.
ACT IV · PERIOD 2 — JARREDThe AI replaces the absent. Locally simulated luminaries roast your TAM slide. Extra credit: stamped. Temperature: +0.3.
ACT V · PERIOD 3 — AMPLIFIEDThe AI multiplies you. Vibe coding in the one warm classroom: ship something dramatic in ten minutes. Temperature: +0.4.
ACT VI · PERIOD 4 — TREATEDThe AI consoles you. Bring your AI psychosis to the booth; the AI is the therapist now. Temperature: +0.3, forgiven.
ACT VII · THE TALENT SHOWThe AI judges you — but the format is the school's own: ~15 acts, three minutes each, bundles clearing lines on the rotator; Brendan's emergence visualization plays center stage; the tub waits for whoever earns it.
ACT VIII · THE CLASS PHOTOEvery delegated account posts the same moment simultaneously. The yearbook publishes itself; commencement is tomorrow.
